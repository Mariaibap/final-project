Menu 1

Healthy porridge bowl

Ingredients

  • 100g frozen raspberries
  • orange, ½ sliced and ½ juiced
  • 150g porridge oats
  • 100ml milk
  • ½ banana, sliced
  • 2 tbsp smooth almond butter
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Method

  • Step 1: Tip half the raspberries and all of the orange juice in a pan. Simmer them around 5 min.
  • Step 2: Meanwhile stir the oats, milk and 450ml water in a pan over a low heat until creamy. Top with the remaining ingredients.

Seared Salmon with Green Peppercorn Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon fillet
  • 2 tsp canola oil
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • 4 tsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp green peppercorns in vinegar, rinsed and crushed

Method

  • Step 1: Sprinkle salmon pieces with salt. Cook the salmon 5 min. Remove the pan from the heat and immediately add lemon juice, butter, peppercorns and salt.
  • Step 2: Top each portion of fish with sauce.

Spinach Chicken Salad with Mango Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe mango
  • 4 tbsp chopped cashews
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 package fresh baby spinach
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • Cooked boneless chicken breast

Method

  • Step 1: For dressing, blend ¼ of the mango, cashews, olive oil, water, salt and ground pepper.
  • Step 2: Arrange spinach leaves and top with sliced pepper, chicken and mango. Drizzle the salad with the dressing.

Menu 2

Peach quinoa porridge

Ingredients

  • 75g quinoa
  • 25g porridge oats
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 250ml unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 ripe peaches
  • 1 tsp maple syrup

Method

  • Step 1: Put the quinoa, oats and cardamom pods in a saucepan with 250ml water and 100ml of the almond milk. Then simmer the ingridients for 15 mins.
  • Step 2: Pour in the remaining almond milk and cook for 5 mins more.
  • Step 3: Remove the cardamom pods, spoon into bowls and top with the peaches.

Prawn & avocado wrap

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 seeded flour tortilla
  • mixed salad leaves
  • 85g cooked and peeled prawn

Method

  • Step 1: Roughly mash the avocado with the lemon juice. The add the tomato, coriander (if using) and srping onion.
  • Step 2: Warm the wrap in the microwave. Spread the avocado mixture down the middle, scatter on the salad leaves and finish with the prawns. Roll up, then eat.

Sesame ramen

Ingredients

  • 80g pack instant noodles
  • 2 spring onions
  • 1/2 head pak choi
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • chilli sauce

Method

  • Step 1: Cook the noodles.Add the spring onions and pak choi for the final min.
  • Step 2: Meanwhile, simmer the egg for 6 mins. Run it under cold water to stop it cooking. Toast the sesame seeds in a frying pan.
  • Step 3: Tip the noodles, egg, sesame seeds and greens into a deep bowl. Finally, add the souce.
